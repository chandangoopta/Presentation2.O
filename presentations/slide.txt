Presentation **2.O**
Next generation Presentation
* Prepare slides simply from a text (**.txt**) file
* **Saves** much of your **time**, as the **focuses** on preparing **contents** rather than preparing slides 

Features
[[mobile.png]]
* Take control of slides from any device
* Presentation without projector
* **Easy** preparations
* errors/crashes/requests **logs**

Future enhancements
* Availability of **Themes**/**Templates**/**Transitions**
* Export slides to **PDF**
* Limit requests
* Q/A in real time
* **Audio**/**Video** support
* More **Standarized** parsing

Requirements
* **Web server** running **php**
* and a **browser**

The idea
* Timer that makes **requests** for slide status **every second** (jquery, ajax)
* Checks which slide to show (php)
* **Parse** the **text file** and generates **json** (php)
* **Displays**/**changes** slide at client end (jquery, ajax, json)

Design
* Responsive UI
* Transitions
